## mmWaves
-> In this fast world,mmWaves came into picture.This Application such as 4k straming,VR,cloud gaming which all need "huge 
   bandwidth" and low delay which can be provided by high frequency waves.
-> High frequency Waves results into lower wavelength which are known as mmWaves (millimeter Waves)

## Disadvantage of mmWaves
-> High Attenuation 
   High Attenuation means loosing its strength while travelling .The received power decreases with distance because the 
   transmitted energy spreads over large area
   Think of it like this :
   ->A large Ocean Wave can travel a long distance before dying out
   ->Tiny ripples disappear much more quickly
   mmWave signals behave more like those tiny ripples
-> Short Range 
   High Attenuation Results in Short Range
-> Poor Wave Attenuation
   When a Radio Wave Hits a Wall,Reflection,Absorption,Transmission takes place
   1. At lower frequencies loner wavelengths pass through walls more easily and less energy absorbed
   2. At mmWave frequencies the wavelength is very is small,more energy is absorbed and much less energy passes through

## Advantage of mmwaves
Despite all the disadvantages above we prefer mmWaves because it offers a very large bandwidth.More bandwidth means much 
higher possible data rates

## Hybrid Beamforming
beamforming is a process of directing beam in a particular direction rather than into a free space
-> Hybrid Beamforming is type of beamforming which uses both Analog and Digital Beamforming
-> When the waves coming from m antennas are added together constructively ,creating a stronger signal.This is called 
 beamforming gain or array gain
-> It combines the advantage of digital beamforming of best performance(each antenna has its owm RF chain) and Analag 
Beamforming of Low cost and Low power (m antennas share only 1 RF chain)
-> Hybrid beamforming increase Spectral efficiency 
Spectral Efficiency defines how many bits of information can be transmitted per second for each hertz of bandwidth.Higher 
SE means better use of the available spectrum.With Beamforming Signal becomes strong and snr will be high due to which more 
data streams can be transmitted reliably,errors decreases ,As a result of it Spectral efficiency increases

## How Hybrid beamforming solves the issues of mmWaves
1.Attenuation: Hybrid beamforming focuses energy towards the receiver,increasing received power 
2.Low SNR results in high spectral efficiency.
3.Poor Wall penetrations cannot be avoided but a stronger beam helps maintain links when there is a clear path
